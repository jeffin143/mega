Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Megaplay

  • What is Megaplay?

    Megaplay is the annual thanksgiving event of Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences.

  • Why is Megaplay special?

    Because it is the most anticipated event of the year and one of its kind in South India, organized by Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences, Coimbatore.

  • What is Megaplay about?

    It is a Mega programme organized to celebrate Christmas season among the staff members and student community of the institute.

  • When is Megaplay ’18?

    It is planned to be held on the 14th of December 2018.

  • Who handles this event?

    DOVE department of Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences.

  • Where is the venue for Megaplay ’18?

    It will be held at Bro. D.G.S Dhinakaran Auditorium.

  • Who all can participate in Megaplay ’18?

    Students pursuing their UG and PG courses except UG first years of Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences can participate in Megaplay.

  • Who can attend the Megaplay?

    All the students, their family and Alumni of Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences can attend Megaplay.

  • Will it be aired?

    Yes, absolutely.

  • If I am not able to attend the Megaplay, how can I watch it online?

    You can watch the event live on YouTube and Facebook
