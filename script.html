Let’s get to know more about the Woody Allen and Diablo Cody of the Megaplay. The three things that are needed for a marvellous skit is the script, the script and the script. They are the masterminds behind an impeccable play. They churn their brains and bring a great influence over the creative direction and emotional impact. Scriptwriters are the official puppeteers of the play.

They have to pull all the right strings to be able to script it right. They are responsible for researching the story, developing the narrative, writing the script, dialogues and delivering it in the required format to the developers. Our karunyans have put forth an astounding team of scriptwriters who are about to give your eyes a visualizing treat. So sit back and enjoy the glorious, insightful and extraordinary piece of a treat that is our very own Megaplay.
