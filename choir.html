About
The Team

“My passion is motivating
people to get results”

The 100 voice choir is a special choir comprised entirely of the students of Karunya, that never fails to give a hearty performance every year. It is the melody of their music that makes them so special. As the name states, this choir features 100 talented singers, both male and female, and a pack of groovy musicians to perform different sets of music fused together, to bring some air to the drama of the Megaplay, and to start the event on a high note.

The choir is led by an experienced choirmaster, that leads the choir and the musicians from one song to another, and all together they form a one-of-a-kind ensemble. The performance is adorned by some jaw-dropping solos, and some rocking musical interludes, that creates a totally different ambience in the place. Surely to deliver such a pleasing performance with seamless transitions, the whole team has to undergo a lot of demo sessions, where they practice with a full dedication to make the final performance error proof.
